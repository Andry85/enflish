Entering for the Cambridge ESOL Examinations

Individual Candidate Registration | Supplier School Registration

*The closing date for private candidates and supplier schools has now passed for the FCE, CAE, BEC, PET and KET examinations for March 2007. The late fee of £10 is now applicable and late registration will not be processed without it.*

Dear Candidate

If you would like to enter for one of the Cambridge ESOL examinations in 2007 you should now download one of the ESOL Individual Registration Forms below and follow these instructions.

On this registration form you should ensure that you state the following correctly:

Examination Session - the date or session for which you would like to be entered
Full name - the name that you would like to appear on your certificate
Address - the address at which you would like your Statement of Entry to be sent
Date of Birth - In the format dd:mm:yy.
Signature & Date

This registration form should also be accompanied by a cheque or postal order for the specified amount (see each individual registration form) made payable to 'UCLES'. We regret that entries without an accompanying cheque or postal order will not be processed. Please ensure that your name and address are clearly and correctly spelt.

Please note that from January 2007 a late fee of £10 will be chargeable after the closing date.

Entry fees are non-refundable unless official proof under medical or other grounds can be provided. A portion of the fee may be refunded under such circumstances. Fees cannot be transferred to a future session.

The form should be completed and returned with the appropriate payment to:

London Open Centre
C/O UCLES
1 Hills Road
Cambridge CB1 2EU

TIMETABLES

You will receive your examination timetable approximately 2 weeks before your examination dates.
You must ensure that you are available on all the days relating to your examination

PROOF OF IDENTITY

All candidates must bring their passport (or the Proof of Identity Form containing at least a photograph, name and signature) and all their timetable documents to every examination. Detailed instructions will follow with your timetable.

If you do not have any current form of identity please click on the link below, complete the form and take it with you to your examinations.


MOBILE TELEPHONES

Please be aware that mobile telephones, pagers and other electronic appliances will not be allowed in the examination hall.

CHANGE OF ADDRESS

Changes of address must be reported in writing immediately as timetables etc. will be sent to the last address submitted.

The registration form should be completed and returned as soon as possible. If you have any further queries please contact the London Open Centre on (01223) 553499 or (01223) 553444 or send an email to londonopencentre@cambridgeesol.org

Please ensure that you are aware of all the requirements and that you are available for the examination dates specified.

It is very important that you take all your timetable documents and identity details to every examination.

Please note: the documents are in PDF format, and you will need the latest version of the Acrobat Reader software in order to open and download them. This can be obtained for free from the Adobe website. The London Open Centre is not responsible for the content of external internet sites.
